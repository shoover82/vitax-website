About VitaX

VitaX is a data-driven initiative that blends personalized health tracking, blockchain incentives, and community engagement. Our platform calculates a Humanity Impact Score (HIS) for each participant by integrating data from wearable devices, telehealth visits, mental health surveys and community involvement. The higher your score, the more Humanity Impact Return (HIR) tokens you earn – turning healthy habits into tangible economic and social benefits.

Core Features

Personalized Health Scoring

We combine daily metrics from devices like Oura and Hume with lab results and mental health questionnaires to provide an integrated HIS score out of 100.

Incentive Token Economy

Earn HIR tokens for improving your HIS. Redeem tokens for community goods or services, creating a virtuous cycle between health and wealth.

Data Sovereignty

Your health data belongs to you. VitaX uses decentralized technology to ensure privacy, consent and secure sharing with healthcare providers and researchers.

Community Engagement

Score points by volunteering and participating in local events. We partner with organizations like Lake Butler Church of Christ to track and reward meaningful service.

Pilot Community

The first VitaX pilot launches in Union County, Florida. We are building a self-sustaining community with affordable housing, telehealth access and a supportive environment for veterans and families.
