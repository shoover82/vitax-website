About VitaX

VitaX is a data-driven initiative that blends personalized health tracking, blockchain incentives and community engagement to promote well‑being and resilience. We believe that everyone deserves access to the tools and support they need to live healthier lives. By integrating biometric data from wearables and lab results with decentralized technology, we empower individuals and communities to own their health data, make informed decisions and earn rewards for positive actions.

Core Features

Personalized Health Scoring

We combine daily metrics from devices like the Oura ring and Hume’s emotion AI with lab results and mental health questionnaires to generate a holistic Health Impact Rating (HIR). This score helps you understand your overall well‑being and track progress over time.

Incentive Token Economy

Earn HIR tokens for improving your health, volunteering and supporting others. Redeem tokens for community goods or services, creating a virtuous cycle of well‑being and generosity.

Data Sovereignty & Security

Your health data belongs to you. VitaX uses decentralized technology, strong encryption and best practices from HIPAA to ensure privacy, consent and secure storage. You decide who can access your information.

Community Engagement

Score points by volunteering and participating in local events. We partner with hospitals, nonprofits and small businesses to create local marketplaces that reward healthy behaviors and social support.

Integrations & Technology

VitaX integrates seamlessly with the Oura API to collect sleep, readiness and activity data, and with Hume’s AI to analyse emotional tone and mental health signals. Our open API allows developers to build applications that respect user consent and privacy while contributing to a richer health ecosystem.

We employ blockchain and encryption technologies to maintain data integrity and transparency. Your Health Impact Rating lives on a secure ledger, and all personally identifiable information is encrypted both in transit and at rest.

Pilot Community

Our first VitaX pilot launches in Union County, Florida. We are building a self‑sustaining community with affordable housing, health services and local economic opportunities. By partnering with Lake Butler Hospital and local clinics, we will test how a token economy can improve access to care, reduce stress and build stronger connections among neighbors.
